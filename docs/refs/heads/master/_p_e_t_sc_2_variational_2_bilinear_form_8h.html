PETSc specialization of variational bilinear forms.

Contents

Provides the partial specialization of Rodin::Variational::BilinearForm that assembles bilinear-form contributions into a PETSc Mat. The resulting matrix represents the stiffness (or system) matrix of a discrete finite element system .
