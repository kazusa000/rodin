Bi-conjugate gradient stabilized solver for non-symmetric sparse systems.

The BiCGSTAB (Bi-Conjugate Gradient Stabilized) method is an iterative solver for non-symmetric linear systems . It combines ideas from the BiCG algorithm with stabilization to avoid the irregular convergence patterns often seen in the Bi-Conjugate Gradient method.

BiCGSTAB is particularly suitable for:

  • Non-symmetric matrices
  • Large sparse systems
  • Problems where direct solvers are too expensive

Example usage:

Problem problem(u, v);
problem = Integral(Grad(u), Grad(v)) + Integral(beta * Grad(u), v) - Integral(f, v);

Solver::BiCGSTAB solver(problem);
solver.setTolerance(1e-10)
      .setMaxIterations(1000)
      .solve();
