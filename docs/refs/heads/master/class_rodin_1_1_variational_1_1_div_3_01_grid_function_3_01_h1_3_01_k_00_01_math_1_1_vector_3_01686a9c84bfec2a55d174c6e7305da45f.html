Divergence of an H1 vector GridFunction.

Computes for vector-valued H1<K> functions. The divergence is polynomial of degree K-1 on each element:

Applications include:

  • Incompressibility constraint:
  • Mass conservation in fluid mechanics
  • Continuity equation
  • Mixed formulations (Stokes, elasticity)
