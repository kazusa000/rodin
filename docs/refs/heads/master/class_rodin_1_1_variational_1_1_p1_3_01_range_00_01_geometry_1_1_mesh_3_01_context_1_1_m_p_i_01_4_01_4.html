Rodin::Variational::P1<Range, Geometry::Mesh<Context::MPI>> class

Distributed P1 finite element space for MPI meshes.

Template parameters
Range Scalar or vector range descriptor used by P1.

Contents

  • Reference

This specialization wraps a local shard P1 space and augments it with distributed global indexing. Owned degrees of freedom receive contiguous global ranges per rank, while ghost dofs are synchronized from owner ranks.

template<class Range>
Rodin::Variational::P1<Range, Geometry::Mesh<Context::MPI>><Range>::P1(const MeshType& mesh)

Constructs a distributed scalar P1 finite element space on the given mesh.

Parameters
mesh in Distributed mesh on which the space is defined.

This constructor builds the local shard P1 space and assigns a global distributed numbering to its degrees of freedom. For scalar P1 elements, degrees of freedom are associated with mesh vertices.

Each rank numbers its owned vertex degrees of freedom contiguously, then exchanges this numbering with neighboring ranks so that ghost vertices are assigned the global indices of their owners. The resulting maps store the correspondence between local shard degrees of freedom and global distributed degrees of freedom.
